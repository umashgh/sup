**overview**
We are building an app to help salaried professionals who are thinking of quitting their jobs and starting their own businesses. These entrepreneurs tend to focus a lot on the business, which tends to maximise the chances of success for the venture. But on the flipside, any events that disrupt their focus may lead to failure of the venture. One major source of such events is the family. If proper planning isn't done for the family, the chances of surprise events (such as a parent falling sick, or a child's education suffering) leading to failure of the venture are high. This tool is designed to help these entrepreneurs plan for their families, and ensure that they are prepared for any eventuality.
